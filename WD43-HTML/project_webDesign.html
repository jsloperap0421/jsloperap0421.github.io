The city of Turin

On this website, you will find information about Turin, the capital of Piedmont. It is a vibrant city with a rich history and a modern atmosphere. Nestled at the foot of the Alps, Turin offers a unique blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty. Known for its elegant architecture, world-class museums, and delicious cuisine, Turin is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Italy.

History

Turin's history dates back to the Roman era, when it was known as Augusta Taurinorum. It later became the capital of the Kingdom of Sardinia-Piedmont, playing a crucial role in the unification of Italy.

Important places

  2. Royal palace of Turin
